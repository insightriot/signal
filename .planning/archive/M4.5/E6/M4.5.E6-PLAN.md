## Phase Goal

Ship a STATE.md schema + auto-update protocol that makes `/sig:resume` an unambiguous, validated picture of where the user left off — even after a full context-clear mid-EXECUTE — so session-to-session handover stops being prohibitively expensive.

**Done when:** All 5 slices shipped; the dogfood criterion (AC#8) demonstrated via a real context-clear during E6's own EXECUTE; release notes call out the schema migration; `M4.5.E6-RESEARCH.md` § 8.1 documents the D15 dry-run result on Signal-on-Signal's own STATE.md.
