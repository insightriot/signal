## Phase goal

Make per-Epic retrospectives a structurally enforced artifact that fails closed under context pressure вҖ” never again skipped because conversation context cleared before SHIP got invoked вҖ” and provide an indexed traversal layer so the captured learning is findable, not buried.

**Non-goals (explicit):** Full wiki restructure of `.planning/` (deferred to M5.E1). Auto-draft retro content from session transcripts (deferred to M5.E1). Dreaming-style cross-Epic synthesis (deferred to M5.E1). Migration tooling for existing projects (deferred to M5.E1). Backfill of past Epics is in scope but only as stubs with `[FILL IN]` markers вҖ” substantive content is opportunistic, not enforced.
