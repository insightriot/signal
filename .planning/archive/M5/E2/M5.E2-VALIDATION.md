## Faithfulness carve-out (the §5 blind spot — do NOT fake it with a test)
`verifyFaithful` proves **token coverage**; conservation proves **no byte loss**. **Neither proves semantic faithfulness** (a card that keeps every ID but paraphrases wrongly). Per D-M5E2-4 + NFR, faithfulness is gated by **S4.t1 dogfood eyeball + the REVIEW panel** — VERIFY must NOT mark it green on unit tests. This is the one AC deliberately not unit-testable; its "test" is the human diff.
