## 7. Plan Deviations — Flagged

Two deviations from the literal PLAN, both surfaced explicitly in commit messages (per "Surface ambiguity, don't resolve silently"). Reaffirmed here for the record:

### 7.1 — Dropped `got` from the bare-word audit pattern (S1.t2)

PLAN acceptance specified `\b(axios|got|node-fetch)\b`. The bare-word `got` produced 13 false-positive hits across `tools/lib/{profile,state}.js` and `tools/validate-plugin.js` because `got` is a pervasive English word in error messages ("got null", "got 'sig'"). `axios` and `node-fetch` aren't English words and stayed in. The `require('got')` and `import ... from 'got'` patterns still catch the `got` module being loaded — which is the actual network-call entry point regardless.

- **Risk:** loss of detection for a `got` instance used without an import statement in the same file. **Real-world likelihood:** zero — JavaScript requires the module to be imported somewhere to be usable.
- **Documented:** in-script comment at `tools/audit-network-calls.js` explaining the omission so a future maintainer doesn't "fix" it by re-adding the bare `got`; also in `bc36985` commit body.
- **Verdict:** acceptable. Surfaced.

### 7.2 — Cited REQUIREMENTS.md instead of DECISIONS.md (S2.t6)

PLAN acceptance for the MILESTONE annotations literally said `**S3–S5 shelved 2026-05-24 — see DECISIONS.md D-E3-12 for trigger to unshelf.**`. But D-E3-10, D-E3-11, D-E3-12, D-E3-1-amend, D-E3-NEW-13, D-E3-NEW-14 currently live in `M4.5.E3-REQUIREMENTS.md`, not in `DECISIONS.md`. To keep the cited destination accurate, the annotations point at REQUIREMENTS.md.

- **Recommended follow-up (out of E3 scope):** promote these decisions to DECISIONS.md per past Epic pattern (M4.5.E6 did this at SHIP time). ~30 minutes of writing. Flagged in `M4.5.E3-PROGRESS.md` § "Recommended follow-ups" too.
- **Verdict:** acceptable. Surfaced. Annotation citations remain accurate at the moment of write.
